Our Mission

Our mission is to bring joy and support to those in need by providing essential services, fostering community spirit, and promoting sustainable development. We strive to create a world where every individual has the opportunity to smile, thrive, and live with dignity. Through education, healthcare, and economic empowerment, we aim to uplift communities and build a brighter, more inclusive future for all.

Our Vision

Our vision is to foster a world where compassion and generosity are the cornerstones of society. We aim to create a global community where every individual is empowered to help others, where donations and acts of kindness are commonplace, and where support systems ensure that no one is left behind. By promoting a culture of giving and solidarity, we strive to build a future where every person can live with dignity, hope, and happiness.

Health Care

Raised : ₹100 / ₹50000

With a population of more than 1.4 Billion, last-mile delivery of healthcare in our country is a big challenge. The geospatial diversity of the country further aggravates the issues of accessibility and availability of healthcare in difficult terrains. With more than 65% of the population residing in rural areas with a smaller share of the overall healthcare infrastructure, and the urban slum dwellers prioritising everyday survival over healthcare, uneven distribution and lack of awareness also limit the uptake of existing healthcare services. It is crucial to address these gaps to meet the goals of Universal Health Coverage and support Govt of India's vision of Ayushman Bharat.
